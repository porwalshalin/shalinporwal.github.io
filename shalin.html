01 — My Story

Where tax mechanics meet
valuation outcomes

My work sits at the intersection of business valuation, tax analysis, and financial modeling. I spend my days spreading tax returns, normalizing owner-level financials, and connecting entity-level tax costs to cash flow and value outcomes. I don't treat tax and valuation as separate disciplines. I treat them as two lenses on the same question: what is this business actually worth, and what does an owner actually take home?

02 — What I Bring

Built on real
valuation work

At VVV Advisors, I spread 3-5 years of 1120S and 1065 filings across closely held business engagements, normalizing for owner compensation, personal expenses, and non-recurring items to derive clean SDE and EBITDA baselines. I analyze working capital trends, flag income-distribution inconsistencies, and assess CFC, GILTI, and PFIC exposures — quantifying how international tax structures affect after-tax cash flows and investor returns. At Baker Tilly, I supported valuation analyses directly, running sensitivity analysis on key assumptions and preparing Excel-based workpapers for senior review.
